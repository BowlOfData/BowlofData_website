About Bowl of Data

A weekly digest at the intersection of AI, security, blockchain, and engineering: curated by humans, powered by Maki.

Our Mission

Staying current with technology should take minutes, not hours. Every week, hundreds of articles, papers, and announcements compete for your attention: most of them are noise. We sort through it so you don't have to, selecting only the stories that genuinely matter and packaging them into a tight, readable digest.

Our focus is deliberate: breakthroughs in AI and machine learning, exploits that reshape security, meaningful shifts in the blockchain ecosystem, and engineering practices worth adopting. No hype, no noise; just the signal.

How It Works

01

Collect

Maki, our AI agent orchestration framework, scans hundreds of sources every week: RSS feeds, research repositories, security advisories, and engineering blogs. Every relevant signal is captured before curation begins.

02

Summarise

Each article receives a concise TL;DR and a deeper contextual analysis, generated by the pipeline and refined for clarity. The bar is consistent: every item has to earn its place, and nothing ships if it doesn't add value.

03

Publish

The team reviews, curates, and ships the final digest each week: here on the site and on Substack. Human judgement is always the last gate before anything goes out.

Get involved with Maki

Maki is currently in private development: the repository is not yet public, as the project is still in its early stages. That said, we believe in building with the right people from the start, and collaboration is already open.

Whether you are a developer interested in contributing to the framework, a technical writer who wants to document AI agent systems, or a reviewer who can help sharpen the newsletter's quality: send a request through the contact page and we will get back to you.
